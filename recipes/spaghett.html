Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This easy spaghetti and meat sauce recipe takes just a few minutes of prep and then a low simmer on the stove for a deep, meaty-flavored, homemade spaghetti for the best family favorite dinner.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1lb of Ground Beef
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil pot of water and add spaghetti cook till tender
  2. Brown ground beef and add pasta sauce to pan once meat is cooked
  3. Eat
